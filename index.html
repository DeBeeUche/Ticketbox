#CanYoufeelrome 2025
Meet & Greet with Can Yaman
Villa Monte d'Oro Rome | Italy

|Sat 19 July 2025 | 19:00

Rome is preparing for a night that goes beyond the ordinary. A night to remember!
On July 19th, 2025, fans will have the opportunity to take part in a unique experience with Can Yaman, under the stars of the Eternal City.
The event, titled Meet and greet #CanYoufeelrome, promises an evening full of energy, emotion and elegance - blending presence, connection, and curated moments that will stay with you long after the night ends.
The night will include a refined atmosphere, an exclusive buffet with drinks, and selected fan interactions, making the audience not just spectators, but part of the story.
